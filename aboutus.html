Pinder’s mission is to help get pets adopted with loving owners because as of right now there is not a large platform to find one’s true pet. The long term goals are to expand Pinder to other shelters around the globe and give more pets a better chance of getting adopted. So far we have a website and app for download for pets and pet lovers to meet. Pinder is unique because it provides the average person a platform to find a potential pet. By downloading our app, people can view potential pets and talk to Pinder consultants to learn more about the animals. If they are interested in adopting, they can set up a date to meet with both the consultant and the pet. Pinder’s policy is to charge only after the customer is completely satisfied and ready to adopt their pet! Risks include having glitches in the app, but we will assure this does not happen by hiring professional app designers. Since Pinder is mostly an online business, there is always the risk of being hacked.
