Our Mission:

- Pinder’s mission is to help get pets
adopted with loving owners

- The long term goals are to expand
Pinder to other shelters around
the globe and give more pets a better chance
of getting adopted.

Why We Do What We Do:

- Thousands of pets are Euthanized every year because
no one is there to adopt them. We knew we could not let
these adorable, deserving pets die when ther are so many
loving families out there that could stop this cruelty!

- So far we have a website and app for download for pets
and pet lovers to meet. Pinder is unique because it provides
the average person a platform to find a potential pet. By
downloading our app, people can view potential pets and talk
to Pinder consultants to learn more about the animals. If they
are interested in adopting, they can set up a date to meet with
both the consultant and the pet. Pinder’s policy is to charge only
after the customer is completely satisfied and ready to adopt their
pet! Risks include having glitches in the app, but we will assure
this does not happen by hiring professional app designers. Since
Pinder is mostly an online business, there is always the risk
of being hacked.
